Censorship warning plugin

Be warned of zensorship and surf the Internet with confidence. This is an experiment and will be improved over time.

Get Started

What it does

The main goal of this extension is to give users the ability to consciously shape their online experience and make informed decisions about what information they want to consume.

Reliable source

By integrating data sources that provide information about censored sites, analyzing content and notifying users of potential censorship, the extension aims to provide a proactive solution to promote freedom of information and strengthen democracy.

Faster detection and protection

A key feature is the detection of censored content by integrating algorithms and data sources that can identify censored websites and content. If a user visits a censored page or encounters censored content, they will be immediately informed about it through a notification. This warning is intended to alert the user that certain information may be blocked or restricted and to allow them to take further action.

Attractive design

Another goal of this browser extension is to provide user-friendly views with dashboards and metrics on users' personalized content to better understand the mechanisms and effects of censorship.
